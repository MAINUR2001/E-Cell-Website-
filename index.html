Event Highlights

SRIJAN

The Annual flagship event of the Enterprenuership Cell NIT Silchar in association with the Institute Innovation Cell (IIC). This is a 3-day Entrepreneurial fest filled with interactive sessions, guest lectures, informal events, and sagacious workshops that aim to provide a platform that stimulates discussions and promotes interest in Entrepreneurship amongst the young minds, and motivates them to unleash their brimming potentials.

EMPRESSARIO

An annual business module that includes a variety of inclusive contests ranging from designing creative taglines to innovative Business ideas like convincing someone to buy even something trivial (Eg.: An empty matchbox !). It falls under the ostentatious Techno-Managerial fest-Technoesis, organised by the Entrepreneurship Cell NIT Silchar, to espouse ingenuity and managerial aspects amongst the students.
